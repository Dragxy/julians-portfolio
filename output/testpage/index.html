About Me

Hi there, I'm Julian! :)

If you've made it this far, you probably already know my name—but I’m glad you’re here! Whether you’re exploring my portfolio out of curiosity or searching for collaboration opportunities, I want to thank you for taking the time to visit.

I’m a software developer with a passion for creating, experimenting, and solving challenges. My journey into the world of technology began at the age of 10, tinkering with multiplayer servers, installing mods, plugins, and diving into the endless possibilities of customization.

The real turning point for me came in the summer of 2020, just before starting my studies at HTL Saalfelden, an IT-focused school in Salzburg. That summer, I discovered how much magic a little code and a lot of imagination could bring to my favorite sandbox game. Since then, I’ve never looked back.

Over the years, I’ve grown immensely, both personally and professionally. While my skills and experiences have expanded, one thing has remained constant: my love for experimenting, problem-solving, and contributing to something larger than myself. Back then—and still today—if you asked me why I love computer science, I’d say it’s about finding creative, innovative ways to develop features, collaborating with talented teams, and putting together the pieces of a much bigger puzzle.

I’ve developed expertise in object-oriented and process-oriented programming, network setup, server management, and web component design. For a deeper dive into my technical experience, feel free to explore my portfolio and the projects I’ve showcased.

Whoever you are and whatever brought you here, I hope you’ve found what you’re looking for. If you have an exciting project or idea and think I could add value, I’d be thrilled to work with you or your team.
