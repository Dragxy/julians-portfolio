A World Without Computer Science is Unimaginable!

We offer you a comprehensive education in all aspects of software development, network technology, databases, artificial intelligence, as well as business management and project management. After graduating from the HTL for Computer Science, an incredibly wide range of career opportunities awaits you.

Computer Architecture and Operating Systems

How is a computer structured? What tasks does an operating system perform? How do I install applications? How do interfaces work? This course teaches the foundational principles of computer science.

Programming and Software Engineering

This subject covers the core competencies of a modern computer science education. It begins with essential programming techniques and progresses to real-world tasks, guiding students through all stages of software development using current platforms.

Databases

Databases form the backbone of countless applications. Students learn to develop, program, and implement various types of databases and practice these skills through hands-on projects.

Network Systems and Cybersecurity

This area focuses on the design and implementation of communication networks. Students plan and create computer networks using suitable components such as LAN cables, switches, routers, firewalls, and WLAN systems. Practical lessons cover how to install, configure, and secure network services like web servers.

Web Programming and Mobile Computing

Learn to develop websites and create your own apps across various application domains, such as games, sports, and the Internet of Things.

Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

“Big Data” and “Artificial Intelligence” are the key topics of this subject, preparing students for cutting-edge fields.

Business Management and Leadership

This course provides essential knowledge about the operational areas of businesses, their organization, and management.

Empowering Women in Computer Science

For girls interested in technology and seeking careers beyond traditional roles, computer science offers an exciting path. With opportunities in all areas of technology—from game design to advanced databases—the field is especially attractive. This education equips students for the workforce, offering excellent pay and growth opportunities.

The future is waiting for you—become a part of it!
